# Skill: write-content

Durable, hand-written content is the strategic bet. Few, deep, on-voice, human-finished. Compose the **writing-voice** skill for all prose.

## What this covers
- **Site review pages** (`/sites/[slug]`): a hand-written review + a live raffle widget pulling that site's current raffles.
- **Pillar guides** (clusters: trust/legal, value/strategy, commercial, enthusiast — see docs/CONTENT.md).

## What this does NOT cover (CLAUDE.md rules 3–6)
- No per-raffle pages. No mass/loop generation. No auto-publish. AI may draft; a human edits and approves before publish.

## Procedure
1. **Pick a real query/intent.** Each page answers a distinct thing people actually search. If it overlaps an existing page, expand that page instead of making a thin new one. Quality over quantity — a thin/duplicative page is a Google risk, not a win.
2. **Outline answer-first.** Each section opens with a direct 40–60 word answer, then detail.
3. **Draft in voice.** Use writing-voice. Show real numbers; use our odds/value/EV framing; stay honest and responsible.
4. **For site reviews:** cover what the site is, its model, trust signals (Companies House, Trustpilot, Voluntary Code, draws, free route, payout history), who it suits, honest pros/cons — then the live widget. Keep prose accurate and dated.
5. **Interlink.** Guides link to relevant site pages and to each other (hub-and-spoke); site pages link to relevant guides. This is what makes pages rank.
6. **AEO pass:** FAQ/Article (and Product/Offer where relevant) JSON-LD; visible "last updated"; current-year title signals; named author; link to the methodology page.
7. **Compliance pass** (COMPLIANCE.md checklist): 18+/responsible framing; no fake urgency; affiliate disclosure if links present; significant conditions stated; accurate odds/prizes.
8. **Human review → publish.** Never auto-publish.

## Voice reminder
Match the live `/how-it-works` and `/about` pages. Honest, plain, real car knowledge, no hype, no thought-leader tone, UK English. The voice is the moat.

## Acceptance
- Distinct intent, genuinely useful, not thin/duplicative.
- Answer-first; on-voice; interlinked; AEO elements present; named author.
- Compliance checklist passed.
- Human-edited and approved before going live.
