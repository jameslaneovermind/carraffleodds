## How It Works

- **Model:** Fixed-odds prize draws (skill-based to comply with UK law)
- **Ticket prices:** £0.10–£24.97; most standard competitions 97p per ticket
- **Draw types:**
  - Live draws: Facebook livestream using Google's Random Number Generator
  - Auto draws: Trigger when tickets sell out or timer expires
  - Instant wins: "Every ticket wins" — email/site credit notification immediately
- **Guarantee:** At least one winner per draw
