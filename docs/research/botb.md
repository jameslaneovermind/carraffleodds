## Competition Model

**Spot the Ball (skill-based):**
- Players mark where a football's centre should be on a photo with the ball removed
- Independent panel of professional sporting experts determines actual position, in presence of independent lawyer — recorded and published
- Closest entry wins; if tied, next-closest decides
- Runs Mon–Sun; results announced Tuesday
- Max 75 tickets per person per week
- Verified by **PromoVeritas** (UK's leading independent promotional verification service)

**Other competition types:**
- House & luxury car: random draw, 1,000 ticket limit
- Lifestyle: random draw, runs Tue–Mon, results Thursday, 1,000 ticket limit
- Instant wins: auto-selected with qualifying purchases

**Ticket prices:** 10p–£6 depending on competition (many in 40p–90p range)

## Free Entry Route

- Postal entry (handwritten postcard/letter) to: 2 Plato Place, 72-74 St Dionis Road, London, SW6 4TU
- Up to 600 postal entries per person per competition
- Must have active BOTB account matching entry details
- **No free entry for Spot the Ball** (skill competition, exempt from free-entry requirement)
- Equal odds as paid entry for eligible competitions
