## How It Works

- **Model:** Fixed-odds prize competitions (skill question — trivial multiple choice)
- **Draw types:**
  - **Live draws:** Monday and Thursday at 8:10 PM, streamed on Facebook/Instagram/YouTube using professional ball machines (lottery-grade equipment)
  - **Auto draws:** Triggered on sellout or scheduled date using RNG algorithm
- **Ticket prices:** 99p–£50 (early bird discounts; price rises after early period)
- **Guaranteed:** All prizes drawn on stated date or earlier if sold out

## Free Entry Route

- Digital: submit contact details only (no purchase)
- Postal: unenclosed postcard to registered address with name, address, DOB, phone, email, prize name, answer
- One ticket per person; duplicates removed
- Must arrive before deadline; Rev Comps not responsible for Royal Mail delays

## Notable Issues / Watch Points

- **Website security:** October 2024 audit found WordPress 6.3.x (1+ year outdated), 28+ plugins severely behind — "genuine security risk" for users entering payment/personal data. Check if this has been remediated.
- **No responsible gambling page** — no deposit limits, self-exclusion, or Begambleaware integration
- **Auto draw opacity** — mechanics not clearly explained; winners not displayed with same transparency as live draws
- **Email spam** — unsolicited emails about undersold competitions widely complained about
- **Compliance gap** — described as "probably least compliant" with Gambling Commission guidance; trivial skill question may not meet GC standards
- **Free entry visibility** — buried in navigation rather than prominently displayed
- **Winvia acquisition** — shares parent with BOTB and Click Competitions post-deal
