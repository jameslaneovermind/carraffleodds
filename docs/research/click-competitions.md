- **Positive themes:** Affordable tickets (29p–£9.95), fast prize payouts (often within 24 hours, 21 calendar days guaranteed), transparent live draws, diverse prize selection, good customer service responsiveness, free postal entry available

## Competition Model

- **Model:** Fixed-odds draw (mandatory trivia question for legal compliance)
- **Ticket prices:** 10p–£9.95; most commonly 29p–£1.99
- **Draw types:**
  - Live draws: Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Saturdays — streamed on Facebook/Instagram
  - Instant win: 24-hour cycles
  - All draws guaranteed on scheduled dates; no extensions
- **Ticket limits:** Max 100 entries per person per online competition; postal max 600
- **Odds:** Not labeled "fixed odds" explicitly, but calculable — total/sold tickets displayed per competition; players can compute their own probability

## Free Entry Route

- Postal entry (handwritten, standard industry terms apply)
- Full Terms & Conditions contain the details
- Same odds per entry as paid entries

## Winner Verification

- Live draws streamed on Facebook/Instagram using Google RNG
- Ticket numbers visible during draw
- Winners contacted directly; photographic ID required for verification
- Cash payouts within 21 calendar days guaranteed (typically 24 hours)
- Winners' page on website with photos/testimonials
- "Thousands of winners paid since 2020"
