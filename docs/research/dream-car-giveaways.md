## Trustpilot

- **Rating:** 4.0 / 5 (also 4.7 on Trustguide.ai from 71 reviews — conflicting, use Trustpilot as primary)
- **Review count:** 8,200+ reviews
- **Positive themes:** Transparent live draws, fast payouts (within hours to 15 hours), friendly staff, quality prize fulfilment, easy UX, genuine winners confirmed
- **Criticisms:** Suspected repeat winners (same person won Audi RS6, BMW M3 + property within days); winners page selective (only photo testimonials); persistent marketing contact post-signup; unable to view complete historical draw data; data sharing concerns (third-party gambling sites)

**Controversy:** "Dominic" won 3 major prizes within 1–2 weeks. Company response: mathematically possible as independent draws. Company flagged some critical Trustpilot reviews as "containing illegal or harmful content" — raises transparency concerns.

## Competition Model

- **Model:** Fixed-odds competition (skill question for legal compliance — trivial multiple choice)
- **Ticket prices:** 9p–£2.60; most in 29p–£1.99 range; marketing emphasises "from just 99p"
- **Ticket limits:** Typically 3,000–10,000 total tickets per competition; per-person cap varies by competition
- **Odds:** Fixed — odds calculable from tickets available/sold; typically 1:3,000 to 1:10,000 per ticket
- **Draw frequency:** Multiple per week; app features live draws Friday and Sunday nights; "new prizes added daily"

## Free Entry Route

- **Postal address:** DCG Ltd, PO Box 2050, Pershore, WR10 9FA
- Include: competition details, full name, DOB, address, phone, email, answer
- Account required (must match postal entry)
- Multiple entries allowed up to competition limit (each posted separately; bulk = single entry)
- Sliding scale by competition price:
  - 49p competition → 2 entries per postage stamp
  - 29p competition → 3 entries
  - 9p competition → 10 entries
- Entries received within 48h of deadline: transferred to next similar-value competition at promoter's discretion
- Hand-delivered entries not accepted

## Winner Verification

- Live draws: human presenters on Facebook/YouTube; RNG via PHP's `random_int()`; all draws streamed
- Cash prizes: Deposited directly to nominated bank account (as fast as 15 hours reported)
- Vehicles: Delivered after identity/eligibility verification
- Notarized affidavit required for conditional winners
- Company claims: "£155–£176M distributed" (varies by source/date), "135,000–159,000 winners"
- **Transparency concern:** Winners page only shows highlight-reel cases with photos/testimonials; no ability to independently verify complete winner list
