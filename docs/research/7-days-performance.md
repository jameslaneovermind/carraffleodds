## How It Works

- **Model:** Fixed-odds prize draw (skill question for legal compliance — straightforward multiple choice)
- **Ticket prices:** 20p–£1; most main draws ~50p; bulk discounts available
- **Guaranteed winners:** Every week regardless of sellout — no rollovers
- **Draws:** Live every Monday at 8pm on Facebook and YouTube using Google RNG
- **Odds:** Transparently shown before purchase; ticket count displayed for odds calculation

## Free Entry Route

- Daily free draw via mobile app (no payment required)

- Free draw prizes limited vs paid competitions
- Optional £1 upgrade offered post-win (not mandatory)
